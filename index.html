About

20 years of visual effects.
Credits on some of the most
celebrated animated films ever made.

Jay Han is a Compositing Supervisor based in Vancouver with two decades of experience in Hollywood VFX and feature animation. His work spans Academy Award–winning productions including Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse and Avatar — films that redefined what animation could look and feel like.

Beyond the studio, Jay teaches digital compositing and actively contributes to the VFX community. In 2025 he presented on K-Pop Demon Hunters at SIGGRAPH Asia, exploring the intersection of authentic cultural storytelling and next-generation pipeline techniques.
