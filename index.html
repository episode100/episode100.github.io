About

20 years of visual effects.
Credits on some of the most celebrated animated films ever made.

Jay Han is a Compositing Supervisor based in Vancouver with two decades of experience in Hollywood VFX and feature animation. His work spans Academy Award–winning productions including Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse and Avatar.

Beyond the studio, Jay teaches digital compositing and actively contributes to the VFX community, presenting at SIGGRAPH Asia on the intersection of cultural storytelling and next-generation pipeline techniques.
